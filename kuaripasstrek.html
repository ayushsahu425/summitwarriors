6-Day Expedition Map

01
Rishikesh to Dhak / Karchi

255KM drive (9-10 hrs). Watch for the Panch Prayag (confluences) along the way.

02
Dhak / Karchi to Akhrotghetta

2.75 KM trek (3 hrs). Forest ascent with views of Mt. Dronagiri.

03
Akhrotghetta to Khullara

2.5 KM (4 hrs). Walk through Golden Oaks and Rhododendrons; catch the Alpenglow.

04
Khullara to Tali via Kuari Pass

7.65 KM (8-9 hrs). Summit Kuari Pass for grand Himalayan views followed by a ridge walk.

05
Tali to Auli & Drive to Karchi

8.1 KM trek via Gorson Bugyal. View Mt. Nanda Devi, then drive (1 hr) back to Karchi.

06
Return to Rishikesh

9-10 hour drive (255 KM). Early 6:00 AM start to conclude the expedition.

Kuari Pass Trek: The Lord Curzon Trail

12,760 FT

Altitude

18-20 KM

Trek Distance

Moderate

Difficulty

Experience the "Lord Curzon Trail" in the heart of the Garhwal Himalayas. This 6-day expedition offers mesmerizing views of India's second-highest peak, Nanda Devi, along with Dronagiri, Kamet, and Hathi-Ghoda peaks. The trail takes you through ancient forests of blue pine and golden oak, opening up to the vast Gorson Bugyal meadows.

Expedition Itinerary

1
Rishikesh to Dhak / Karchi (Drive)

Drive: 255 KM | 9-10 Hours.
Altitude: 7,785 ft.
Watch out for the famous Panch Prayag of Uttarakhand during your drive. Overnight stay at Dhak / Karchi.

2
Dhak / Karchi to Akhrotghetta (Trek)

Trek: 2.75 KM | 3 Hours.
Difficulty: Easy-Moderate.
A lovely day's trek through oak and maple forests with Mt. Dronagiri standing tall throughout the day.

3
Akhrotgetta to Khullara (Trek)

Trek: 2.5 KM | 4 Hours.
Difficulty: Easy.
Walk through golden oaks and Rhododendrons. Watch out for the spectacular alpenglow from the Khullara campsite.

4
Khullara to Tali via Kuari Pass (Trek)

Trek: 7.65 KM | 8-9 Hours.
Highlights: Grand views of the Greater Himalayas. Enjoy a ridge walk and end the day with a serene walk through Tali forest.

5
Tali to Auli (Trek) & Drive to Dhak / Karchi

Trek: 8.1 KM | 7-8 Hours | Drive: 21 KM.
Trek via Tali Lake and Gorson Bugyal with stunning views of Mt. Nanda Devi. Descent through Padiyar forest before driving back to Karchi.

6
Dhak / Karchi to Rishikesh (Drive)

Drive: 255 KM | 9-10 Hours.
Start early (preferably before 6:00 AM) for the long journey back to Rishikesh, revisiting the Panch Prayags along the way.
