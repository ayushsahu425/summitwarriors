Kuari Pass Trek: The Lord Curzon Trail

12,760 FT

Altitude

18-20 KM

Trek Distance

Moderate

Difficulty

Experience the "Lord Curzon Trail" in the heart of the Garhwal Himalayas. This 6-day expedition offers mesmerizing views of India's second-highest peak, Nanda Devi, along with Dronagiri, Kamet, and Hathi-Ghoda peaks. The trail takes you through ancient forests of blue pine and golden oak, opening up to the vast Gorson Bugyal meadows.

Expedition Itinerary

1
Rishikesh to Karchi (Drive)

Drive: 250 KM (approx 9-10 hrs)
Pickup: 6:00 AM at Rishikesh Tapovan.
Arrival at Karchi village. Overnight stay in a local guest house or homestay.

2
Karchi to Khullara (Trek)

Trek: 5-6 KM | 5-6 Hours.
Ascend through forests of Blue pine and Golden oak. Lunch on the trail. Camp setup near Kuari Pass base with views of Mt. Dronagiri.

3
Khullara to Kuari Pass Summit (Summit Day)

Trek: 7 KM Round Trip.
Early morning ascent (Start ~9 AM). Witness the 360-degree panorama of the Garhwal peaks. Return to base camp for hot lunch and evening relaxation.

4
Khullara to Karchi (Descent)

Trek: 5 KM | 4-5 Hours.
Descent through beautiful forest meadows. Enjoy a village tour in Karchi and rest for the evening.

5
Karchi to Auli & Joshimath

Visit Auli, the "Skiing Paradise of India". Highlights include Gorson Bugyal (3,519 ft), the artificial lake, and Asia's second-highest ropeway. Overnight stay.

6
Return to Rishikesh

Drive back to Rishikesh. Trip ends with beautiful memories.
