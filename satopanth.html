Expedition Timeline

8-Day Glacial Map

01
Dehradun to Joshimath

300 KM drive (10-11 hrs). Gateway to the Garhwal Himalayas. Overnight in Joshimath.

02
Joshimath to Badrinath

46 KM drive. Acclimatization day. Visit Badrinath Temple and Mana (the last Indian village).

03
Mana to Laxmiban

9 KM trek. View the 400ft Vasudhara Falls and Alaknanda river. Camp in Bhojpatra forests.

04
Laxmiban to Chakrateerth

7 KM trek. Cross Sahastra Dhara (1000 streams). Spectacular views of Mt. Neelkanth.

05
The Holy Lake Push

5 KM reach to Satopanth Lake (14,258 ft). Witness the triangular emerald lake. Return to Chakrateerth.

06
Chakrateerth to Chamtoli

8 KM descent. Retracing the glacial moraines. Camp at Chamtoli meadows.

Satopanth Lake: The Path to Heaven

Moderate+

Difficulty

8 Days

Duration

May-June / Sept-Oct

Best Season

Deep in the Garhwal Himalayas near the base of the Chaukhamba massif lies the sacred, triangular Satopanth Lake. Legend says the Hindu Trinity—Brahma, Vishnu, and Mahesh—meditate at its three corners. This trek is a "mini-expedition" through glacial moraines, offering unmatched views of Mt. Neelkanth, Balakun, and Swargarohini.

Detailed Expedition Itinerary

1
Arrival at Joshimath

Drive from Dehradun/Rishikesh (300 KM). This 10-hour journey takes you through the famous confluences (Prayags) of the Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers.

2
Joshimath to Badrinath

Drive: 46 KM. Spend the day acclimatizing at 10,400 ft. Visit the holy Badrinath Temple and the historic Mana village to see Bhim Pul and the Saraswati River.

3
Trek to Laxmiban

Trek: 9 KM. Following the Alaknanda river, pass the majestic Vasudhara Falls. Camp at Laxmiban, an area lush with Bhojpatra (Birch) trees.

4
Laxmiban to Chakrateerth

Trek: 7 KM. A challenging ascent through boulders and moraines. Pass Sahastra Dhara (Thousand Folds Spring) where water drips through limestone monoliths.

5
Summit: Satopanth Tal

Trek: 5 KM. Reach the crystal-clear emerald lake at 14,258 ft. Absorb the divine silence under the shadow of Mt. Chaukhamba before returning to Chakrateerth.

6
Descent to Chamtoli

Trek: 8 KM. Retrace your steps down the valley. The descent offers a different perspective of the massive Alkapuri glacier snout.

7
Chamtoli to Mana & Joshimath

Trek: 10 KM. Reach Mana village by afternoon and drive back to Joshimath for a well-deserved hot shower and celebration dinner.

8
Return to Dehradun

Drive back to Dehradun/Rishikesh, concluding your spiritual and adventurous journey to the Path of Truth.
